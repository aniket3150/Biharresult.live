How To Apply For Bihar Board Scrutiny Form 2026

  1. Open the official portal https://intermediate.biharboardscrutiny.com or https://intermediate.biharboardonline.com, then click on "Apply for scrutiny (Intermediate Annual Examination 2026)". Register using Roll Code, Roll Number, and Date of Birth to create password.
  2. Login using Roll Code, Roll Number, and Password. Application form for scrutiny will open with candidate details and subjects.
  3. Select specific subject(s) for scrutiny by ticking checkbox for required subject(s).
  4. Click Pay button and complete fee payment through Debit Card, Credit Card, or Net Banking. Submit final form and save acknowledgment.
